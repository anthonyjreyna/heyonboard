All your questions, answered

How fast can we launch?

Most brands are live with a managed recovery system in 14–21 days — we handle recruiting, system build, and rep training in parallel.

Does this replace our Klaviyo flows?

No — it complements them. Flows are great at email, but they can’t pick up the phone, recover a hard decline, or answer the one question keeping a buyer on the fence. We add the human layer on top.

Who actually makes the calls?

Dedicated reps we recruit and train specifically for supplement, beauty, and pet brands — including objection handling and compliance. We manage, QA, and coach them; you don’t hire or manage anyone.

How do you track recovered revenue?

Everything routes through GoHighLevel, wired into Shopify and your subscription platform, so calls, conversions, and recovered dollars are attributed on one dashboard — no manual logging.

What does it cost?

It depends on your volume and which motions you turn on. On the strategy call we build a revenue-leak model from your real numbers and show you the math before you commit to anything.

Is this right for my brand?

It’s built for supplement, beauty, and pet brands doing $1M+ a year in DTC with subscriptions, who know there’s money stuck in abandoned carts and failed payments. If you’re mid-rebrand or under $1M, it’s probably not the moment.
